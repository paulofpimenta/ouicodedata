Bicycle accidents in Paris

In this work, I developped a fully operational web dashboard application to visualize analyzed spatial data of bicycle accidents in Paris from a 10 year period. The backend is served using Flask (Python) and the frontend (dashboard) was developed with Ployty(Dash). More details about now the data was analysed can be found here
